# Unit I: Receptive Field Archaeology

## Research Question

Unit I: Receptive Field Archaeology (ResNet-18/50/152 vs pattern scale)

## Hypothesis

Models with larger effective receptive fields (e.g., deeper ResNet variants) will better capture larger obstacle patterns, while shallower models may retain stronger sensitivity to smaller localized patterns.

## Planned Deliverable

A comparative analysis of ResNet-18, ResNet-50, and ResNet-152 across controlled pattern scales, including qualitative activation/gradient visualizations and quantitative detection metrics.
