# Unit III: Transfer Learning Layer Freezing Strategy

## Research Question

Unit III: Transfer Learning Layer Freezing Strategy (ImageNet→Indian road domain)

## Hypothesis

Progressive or selective layer freezing will improve adaptation stability and sample efficiency compared with fully frozen or fully unfrozen transfer-learning baselines when moving from ImageNet pretraining to Indian road-domain data.

## Planned Deliverable

An ablation study of freezing strategies with training-dynamics tracking and final detection performance comparisons across the five obstacle classes.
